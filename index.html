Bitcoin (BTC)

Bitcoins currently cost about 5 million PHP. It is also known as BTC and is a cryptocurrency. This was first introduced in public in 2009 and is the most well-known and largest cryptocurrency in the world. Learn more about Bitcoin.

Doge Coins (DOGE)

Doge Coin was first created in December 6th of 2013 and it is based off a popular internet meme and it has a shiba inu logo. It inherited many of the same features such as the hashing algorithm, with the only difference being branding and large inflationary supply. It currently costs about 22 PHP today. Ethereum (ETH)

Ethereum is a Proof-of-Stake blockchain that powers decentralized applications (dApps) through smart contracts, without being controlled by a centralized entity. As the first blockchain to feature smart contracts, it has the largest ecosystem of decentralized applications, ranging from decentralized exchanges to crypto lending and borrowing platforms and more.
